In the Corporate Business Recruiting, we are a company with belief that it takes great talent to find great talent and we exhibit persistence in the pursuit of diverse talent pools at AptiQ. AptiQ is looking for an energetic and driven Staffing Specialist to join our team to primarily support Technology Team hiring.

Job Responsibilities

Responsible for full life-cycle recruiting within corporate office, majorly for IT positions Consult with hiring managers to develop a thorough understanding of the position to create a recruiting strategy to support, attract and select top talent. Provide a wow experience to all involved in full life cycle of talent acquisition process and workflow. Partner with organizational development team to ensure recruiting strategies align with succession plans and business priorities.

Qualifications / Skills Required

  • 3-4 years of relevant experience in hiring top Technology specialists like Java, Javascript, Matlab, Machine learning, Angular, Android..
  • Superb customer focus, attention to detail, and organization skills.
  • Ability to interact at all levels
  • Ability to implement targeted sourcing initiatives
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal and cross team collaboration skills.
  • Capable of assessing, defining and implementing forward-looking business hiring strategies within a fast-paced, ambiguous, agile and results-oriented environment.
  • Developing of new features and modifications for Back-end Servers that make up AptiQ Platform.
  • Work with a team of smart front end and back-end developers to develop new functionality in the product.
  • Work with Product Team to understand the change request and suggest best possible ways to implement a feature in quickest possible way.
  • Analyzing a change request and come up with possible ways of implementing the same on the product.
  • 2+ years of Strong knowledge of Java Programming Language.
  • Strong Analytical ability, someone working with Start-up is a preference.
  • Clarity of concepts with SQL Databases; Ability to write SQL queries to interact with SQL Server.
  • Experience in building Java based web application using any framework.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast paced, startup environment
