Frequently Asked Questions

1.At what level of candidate work experience can AptiQ assessments be used?

AptiQ assessments are used at entry level as well as for lateral candidates.

2.Do you have standard assessments or can we customise the assessments?

We have standard tests for various profiles. However, these tests can be customised as per company's job profile and requirements.

3.How are the assessments designed?

The first step is to understand the job profile and map the skills and competencies needed for a candidate to perform well in the job. Post this items that can measure these competencies are identified and accordingly an assessment is designed. An assessment for hiring by an analytics organisation would be very different from the one that is used by a manufacturing organisation. E.g More focus on business problem solving, data analysis, written communication are critical factors for analytics organisations while for a manufacturing organisation, being process focussed, strong attention to details and good grasp on domain fundamentals is critical.

4.How do you decide difficulty level of a question?

The initial difficulty level is assigned by the SME. This difficulty level is constantly checked based on actual data of usage.

5.What is the model of delivery of these assessments?

An internet based delivery is used for delivering these assessments. With an ability to support more than 100,000 concurrent users, the engine is scalable and can be deployed anywhere immediately. The engine is light weight and does not require any additional software to be installed.

6.I want to assess candidates on niche technical/domain subjects. How can you help?

We have a large question bank covering 25+ topics. In case, your requirements are beyond these topics, we should be able to get an assessment designed and delivered to you leveraging our wide network of SMEs. E.g. we supported a client in assessments for Geology/Hydrology

7.How is a benchmarking exercise done?

Check with client on the current paper and cut-off they are using. We have standard papers for each domain/sector. We can share logins with you and candidates within your company can attempt them. Our general recommendation is a cut-off between 40-55%, a more precise one can be determined post the benchmarking exercise. Note: Three factors impact the accuracy of this. One is that the candidates who are giving are all selects so they score higher, second they may be out of practise so it could be lower and third if they don't give sincerely it could be lower.
